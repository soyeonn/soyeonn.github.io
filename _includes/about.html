Welcome to my personal webpage!

I'm a master's student in Computational Clinical Science Lab, Seoul National Unversity. I'm broadly interested in psychiatric disorders, especially in mood and anxiety disorder and their decision-making. I want to figure out the psychological and neurocognitive mechanisms of aberrant decision-making in mood and anxiety disorder. Specifically, by applying computational and neurobiological approaches, I hope to discover (1) underlying cognitive processes of decision-making under mood and anxiety disorder, (2) individuals' risk factors and prognosis, and ultimately enable to provide (3) quantitative and objective index of psychiatric disorders and (4) individualized treatment.
